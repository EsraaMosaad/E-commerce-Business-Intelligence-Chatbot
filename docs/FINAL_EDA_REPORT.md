### 2.3 Feature Engineering: Topic Tags
The Spark pipeline extracted specific topics to enrich the chatbot's RAG capabilities:
*   **Shipping/Delivery**: Mentioned in 22% of reviews (Terms: "arrived", "shipping", "delivery").
*   **Pricing**: Mentioned in 15% of reviews (Terms: "price", "expensive", "value").
*   **Quality**: Mentioned in 34% of reviews (Terms: "quality", "broken", "durability").
*   **Usability**: Mentioned in 12% of reviews (Terms: "easy to use", "design").

## 3. Data Hygiene & Splits
### 3.1 Cleaning Pipeline
*   **Deduplication**: ~45,000 duplicate reviews removed.
*   **Length Clipping**: Reviews clipped to 1,500 characters for LLM context efficiency.
*   **Verified Only**: Pipeline prioritizes verified purchases for higher signal-to-noise.

### 3.2 Data Splits (80/10/10)
Performed by `parent_asin` to prevent product leakage between sets.

## 4. Sample Instruction Format
The data is formatted for **Instruction-Fine-Tuning** as follows:

```json
{
  "system": "You are an expert e-commerce business intelligence analyst...",
  "instruction": "Perform a SWOT analysis for Amazon based on the review below.",
  "context": "Review: The delivery was fast but the product packaging was damaged...",
  "output": "## Strengths\n- Logistics speed\n## Weaknesses\n- Last-mile packaging..."
}
```
